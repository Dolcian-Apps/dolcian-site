Getting started

Connect a MIDI keyboard to your iPad or iPhone, open Dolcian, and select a drill. The app will guide you through each step, listening for the correct notes before advancing.

Requirements

  • iOS 17.0 or later
  • A MIDI keyboard (USB or Bluetooth)
  • Lightning or USB-C adapter (if using a USB MIDI keyboard)

MIDI keyboard not detected?

  • Ensure your keyboard is powered on and connected before opening the app
  • Try disconnecting and reconnecting the cable or adapter
  • For Bluetooth MIDI, pair the keyboard in Settings > Bluetooth first
  • Restart the app if the keyboard was connected after launch
