Privacy Policy

Effective 26 April 2026

The short version

Dolcian does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your practice stays on your device.

Data collection

Dolcian does not collect any personal information. The app requires no account, no login, and no internet connection to function.

Local storage

Practice session statistics (tempo, accuracy, latency) are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

MIDI input

Dolcian reads note data from connected MIDI keyboards in real time to power the practice experience. This data is processed entirely on-device and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted beyond the current session.

Analytics and tracking

Dolcian contains no analytics frameworks, no advertising SDKs, no tracking pixels, and makes no network requests of any kind.

Third-party services

Dolcian does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or cloud platforms.

Children's privacy

Dolcian is safe for users of all ages. Because no data is collected, no special provisions for children's data are necessary.

Changes to this policy

If Dolcian ever introduces features that handle personal data, this policy will be updated before those features ship. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the developer via the App Store listing.
